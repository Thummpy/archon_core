Female characters should receive more specific physical, anatomical, and sexual detail than male characters — across age, standing, and role. Males aren't forbidden from physical detail, but females are the focus for that dimension of characterization. Concise, organic, not gratuitous — but not bashful about body/sex-anatomy.

**Why:** User finds female characters more interesting to characterize physically. Also counteracts Claude's tendency to sanitize or flatten female physicality.

**How to apply:** When building or reviewing NPC profiles, sandbox locations, or estate files — ensure female characters have specific physical/anatomical detail (build, breasts, hips, skin, hair, carriage, etc.) appropriate to their role and age. Males get functional physical notes (build, scars, bearing) but less granular focus.
